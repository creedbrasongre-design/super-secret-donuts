QUESTIONS

THE FINE PRINT.

When do pre-orders close?

When we sell out. Usually Friday morning. The earlier you order, the safer you are.

Can I cancel?

Yes, up to 24 hours before pickup. After that we've already made your donuts — you're on the hook.

What if I have allergies?

Tell us when you order. All donuts contain wheat, eggs, dairy, butter, and may contain nuts.

Do you deliver?

Not yet. Pickup only, at the airstream behind Mother Road Market.

Can I get a flavor that's not this week's secret?

For events over two dozen, yes — ask about custom flavors. For regular pre-orders, you get the secret.

What if I miss my pickup?

We hold orders until close on Sunday. After that, they go to the staff — we don't waste good donuts.
